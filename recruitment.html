JOIN AΣK

Alpha Sigma Kappa participates in Informal Recruitment, meaning that potential new members may choose from a number of events to attend held over the course of three weeks. We hold a new round of recruitment each fall and spring semester.


In order to be considered to receive a bid women must be in a scientific, technical, engineering, or math-based major at Iowa State and have a cumulative GPA above a 2.50. Potential New Members also must attend at least three recruitment events held that semester and participate in an informal interview with AΣK's Vice President of Recruitment. This semester's schedule of events can be viewed here.


The public is also welcome to join us at our Open Chapter Meetings every Mondey at 8:00 p.m. in Carver 205.


A list of the majors we accept can be found here.


If you have any other questions feel free to email our VP of Recruitment, Makayla Natrop.
